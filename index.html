ADHD and insomnia - how are they related? The fact is, the conditions are comorbid and occur together at high rates, yet there's not a lot of research into either causes or treatment. This page is meant to highlight this and serve to educate (in preferably a relatively fun way) about the research done so far on this topic.

Of children between 3 and 12 years old are thought to have ADHD (Salari et. al, 2023)
Of individuals with ADHD are thought to suffer from insomnia (Fadeuilhe et. al, 2021). Coincidentally, this is also the same percentage for ADHD individuals suffering from restless leg syndrome (RLS) (Jan et. al, 2011).
